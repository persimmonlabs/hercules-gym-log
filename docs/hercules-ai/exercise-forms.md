# Exercise Form and Technique Guide

## Chest Exercises

### Barbell Bench Press
Equipment: Barbell, Flat Bench
Primary muscles: Chest, Front Delts, Triceps

Setup:
- Lie flat on bench with eyes under the bar
- Grip bar slightly wider than shoulder width
- Plant feet flat on floor
- Squeeze shoulder blades together

Execution:
1. Unrack bar with arms fully extended
2. Lower bar to mid-chest with control (2-3 seconds)
3. Touch chest lightly, do not bounce
4. Press bar back up in slight arc toward face
5. Lock out arms at top

Common mistakes:
- Bouncing bar off chest
- Flaring elbows too wide (keep at 45-75 degrees)
- Lifting hips off bench
- Uneven grip width

### Dumbbell Bench Press
Equipment: Dumbbells, Flat Bench
Primary muscles: Chest, Front Delts, Triceps

Setup:
- Sit on bench with dumbbells on thighs
- Kick dumbbells up as you lie back
- Position dumbbells at chest level

Execution:
1. Press dumbbells up until arms are extended
2. Lower with control, allowing slight stretch at bottom
3. Keep wrists straight throughout
4. Press back up in slight arc

Common mistakes:
- Dumbbells drifting too far apart at bottom
- Not controlling the descent
- Wrists bending backward

### Incline Dumbbell Press
Equipment: Dumbbells, Incline Bench (30-45 degrees)
Primary muscles: Upper Chest, Front Delts, Triceps

Setup:
- Set bench to 30-45 degree incline
- Position dumbbells at upper chest level

Execution:
1. Press dumbbells up and slightly inward
2. Lower to upper chest with control
3. Maintain constant tension

Common mistakes:
- Bench angle too steep (becomes shoulder press)
- Arching lower back excessively

## Back Exercises

### Barbell Row
Equipment: Barbell
Primary muscles: Lats, Mid Back, Rear Delts, Biceps

Setup:
- Stand with feet hip-width apart
- Hinge at hips, back flat at 45-degree angle
- Grip bar slightly wider than shoulder width

Execution:
1. Pull bar toward lower chest/upper abs
2. Squeeze shoulder blades together at top
3. Lower with control
4. Maintain flat back throughout

Common mistakes:
- Using momentum/jerking the weight
- Rounding lower back
- Standing too upright

### Lat Pulldown
Equipment: Cable Machine, Lat Bar
Primary muscles: Lats, Biceps, Rear Delts

Setup:
- Grip bar slightly wider than shoulder width
- Sit with thighs secured under pads
- Lean back slightly (10-15 degrees)

Execution:
1. Pull bar to upper chest
2. Lead with elbows, squeeze lats at bottom
3. Control the weight back up
4. Full stretch at top

Common mistakes:
- Pulling bar behind neck (injury risk)
- Using momentum/swinging
- Not getting full range of motion

### Seated Cable Row
Equipment: Cable Machine, V-Bar or Straight Bar
Primary muscles: Mid Back, Lats, Rear Delts, Biceps

Setup:
- Sit with feet on footplate, knees slightly bent
- Grip handle with arms extended
- Maintain upright torso

Execution:
1. Pull handle to lower chest/upper abs
2. Squeeze shoulder blades together
3. Control the return, feel the stretch
4. Keep torso stationary

Common mistakes:
- Excessive forward/backward lean
- Rounding shoulders forward
- Using momentum

## Leg Exercises

### Barbell Squat
Equipment: Barbell, Squat Rack
Primary muscles: Quads, Glutes, Hamstrings, Core

Setup:
- Bar on upper back (high bar) or rear delts (low bar)
- Feet shoulder-width or slightly wider
- Toes pointed slightly outward

Execution:
1. Brace core, take a deep breath
2. Descend by pushing hips back and bending knees
3. Go to at least parallel (thighs parallel to floor)
4. Drive through whole foot to stand
5. Exhale at top

Common mistakes:
- Knees caving inward
- Heels rising off floor
- Excessive forward lean
- Not hitting depth

### Romanian Deadlift (RDL)
Equipment: Barbell or Dumbbells
Primary muscles: Hamstrings, Glutes, Lower Back

Setup:
- Stand with feet hip-width apart
- Hold weight with arms straight
- Slight bend in knees (maintain throughout)

Execution:
1. Hinge at hips, pushing them back
2. Lower weight along thighs/shins
3. Stop when you feel hamstring stretch
4. Drive hips forward to return
5. Squeeze glutes at top

Common mistakes:
- Rounding lower back
- Bending knees too much (becomes regular deadlift)
- Not feeling hamstring stretch

### Leg Press
Equipment: Leg Press Machine
Primary muscles: Quads, Glutes, Hamstrings

Setup:
- Sit with back flat against pad
- Feet shoulder-width on platform
- Foot position affects emphasis (higher = more glutes/hamstrings)

Execution:
1. Release safety handles
2. Lower weight with control
3. Go to 90-degree knee bend or slightly past
4. Press through whole foot to extend
5. Don't lock out knees completely

Common mistakes:
- Lower back lifting off pad
- Knees caving inward
- Locking out knees at top
- Bouncing at bottom

## Shoulder Exercises

### Overhead Press (Military Press)
Equipment: Barbell or Dumbbells
Primary muscles: Front Delts, Side Delts, Triceps

Setup:
- Stand with feet hip-width apart
- Grip bar just outside shoulder width
- Bar at collarbone level

Execution:
1. Brace core, squeeze glutes
2. Press bar straight up
3. Move head back slightly as bar passes
4. Lock out overhead
5. Lower with control

Common mistakes:
- Excessive back arch
- Pressing bar forward instead of straight up
- Not bracing core

### Lateral Raise
Equipment: Dumbbells
Primary muscles: Side Delts

Setup:
- Stand with slight forward lean
- Dumbbells at sides, slight elbow bend

Execution:
1. Raise arms out to sides
2. Lead with elbows, not hands
3. Stop at shoulder height
4. Lower with control

Common mistakes:
- Using momentum/swinging
- Raising too high (traps take over)
- Thumbs pointing up (rotate slightly down)

## Arm Exercises

### Barbell Curl
Equipment: Barbell
Primary muscles: Biceps

Setup:
- Stand with feet hip-width apart
- Grip bar shoulder-width, palms up
- Arms fully extended

Execution:
1. Curl bar up keeping elbows stationary
2. Squeeze biceps at top
3. Lower with control
4. Full extension at bottom

Common mistakes:
- Swinging body for momentum
- Moving elbows forward
- Not controlling the descent

### Tricep Pushdown
Equipment: Cable Machine, Rope or Bar
Primary muscles: Triceps

Setup:
- Stand facing cable machine
- Grip attachment with elbows at sides
- Slight forward lean

Execution:
1. Push down until arms are fully extended
2. Squeeze triceps at bottom
3. Control the return
4. Keep elbows stationary

Common mistakes:
- Elbows flaring out
- Using shoulder movement
- Not getting full lockout

### Skull Crusher (Lying Tricep Extension)
Equipment: EZ Bar or Dumbbells, Flat Bench
Primary muscles: Triceps

Setup:
- Lie on bench with arms extended over chest
- Grip bar with narrow grip

Execution:
1. Lower weight toward forehead by bending elbows
2. Keep upper arms stationary
3. Extend back to start
4. Maintain constant tension

Common mistakes:
- Elbows flaring out
- Moving upper arms
- Going too heavy (injury risk)

## Core Exercises

### Plank
Equipment: None (bodyweight)
Primary muscles: Core, Shoulders

Setup:
- Forearms on ground, elbows under shoulders
- Body in straight line from head to heels

Execution:
1. Brace core as if bracing for punch
2. Squeeze glutes
3. Hold position, breathe normally
4. Don't let hips sag or pike up

Common mistakes:
- Hips sagging toward floor
- Hips piking up
- Holding breath

### Cable Crunch
Equipment: Cable Machine, Rope Attachment
Primary muscles: Abs

Setup:
- Kneel facing cable machine
- Hold rope behind head
- Maintain neutral spine at start

Execution:
1. Crunch down by flexing spine
2. Bring elbows toward knees
3. Squeeze abs at bottom
4. Return with control

Common mistakes:
- Pulling with arms instead of crunching
- Hip flexion instead of spinal flexion
- Not controlling the movement
